Volunteering

Co-founder

We Send Hope

Skills: Leadership, Team Collaboration, UX, Graphic Design

I co-founded an organization dedicated to assisting people experiencing homelessness, after hearing about how COVID exacerbated the struggles of homeless people. I successfully worked with my team raising funds to create 30+ care packages to deliver to the homeless in the Boston area containing essential supplies such as first aid kits, towels, rain ponchos, and other toiletries. I also designed our website, which provided me with valuable experience in applying UX design principles essential for frontend development.
